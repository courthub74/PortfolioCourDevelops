About.

From design to development I possess a life long pursuit of making user friendly applications that raise work productivity. Honing my skills in Front End and Back End development, I have exposed myself and become accomplished in several languages and technologies. Dedicated coding is a daily part of my life at home. The challenge of problem solving is why I enjoy coding projects.

My approach is to see the over-arching goal of the companies challenge and to compose the most efficient way there. Through different avenues of research, I stay current in the relevant technologies in web development. It would be my pleasure become part of your team!
